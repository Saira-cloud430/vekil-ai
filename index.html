The process

What it does while you work, not instead of it.

01

02

It scans live listings that match your actual skills, and drafts the proposal in the tone clients respond to — you approve before anything is sent.

Vekil AI answers a client's first questions and clarifies scope. The moment a deal is close, you take over — it never signs on your behalf.
